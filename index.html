I am a macroeconomist working on macroeconomic and forecasting issues at Amazon's Stores Economics and Science (SEAS), based in New York. Previously, I was part of the Forecasting Team of Amazon Web Services (AWS), where I focused on forecasting issues and the connection between macroeconomic developments and the IT sector.

I completed my Ph.D. in Economics from New York University in May 2022. My research focuses on macroeconomics and time series econometrics.


You can contact me at francesco.furno@nyu.edu.
